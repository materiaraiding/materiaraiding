## Difficulty Changes

Changes in difficulty are only noted **in relation** to **Q40**.

(**e.g.** "The **Light**/**Dark Vengeance** debuffs last for **3 minutes**." as opposed to **1 minute** in **Q40**.)

::: details Q15
**General**:
> - The **Light**/**Dark Vengeance** debuffs last for **3 minutes**.
> - **Scourging Blaze** uses the **Exaflare** pattern from the **Normal** mode.
> - **Searing Chains** don't create plus-shaped AoEs shortly after the tethers break.
> - The **towers** don't inflict ^[Bleeding] **Bleeding**, and don't require the **Light Vengeance** debuff to resolve properly.

**Mechanical**:
> - During **Shackles of Greed**, only the ^[Aetheric Mimicry: Healer] **Healer** receives their debuff.
> - The ^[Doom] **Doom** debuff during **Crime and Punishment** lasts for **8 seconds**.
> - There are only **4 Flameborns** during **Fevered Flame**.
:::

::: details Q25
**General**:
> - The **Light**/**Dark Vengeance** debuffs last for **2 minutes**.
> - **Scourging Blaze** uses the **Exaflare** pattern from the **Normal** mode.
> - The **towers** don't inflict ^[Bleeding] **Bleeding**, and don't require the **Light Vengeance** debuff to resolve properly.

**Mechanical**:
> - The ^[Doom] **Doom** debuff during **Crime and Punishment** lasts for **8 seconds**.
> - There are only **4 Flameborns** during **Fevered Flame**.
:::

::: details Q35
**General**:
> - The **Light**/**Dark Vengeance** debuffs last for **2 minutes**.

**Mechanical**:
> - The ^[Doom] **Doom** debuff during **Crime and Punishment** lasts for **6 seconds**.
> - There are only **? Flameborns** during **Fevered Flame**.
:::
